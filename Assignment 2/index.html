Our Mission, Our Promise

Ethical and Beautiful

We are committed to quality products, produced ethically and sustainably with respect for people and planet.
We celebrate the age-old craft skills of our partners and - with the People Tree Foundation - promote initiatives to reinforce the viability of sustainable
community producers. People Tree are proud to be the first international fashion brand certified by the World Fair Trade Association, with all pieces fully
traceable in a transparent supply chain.
