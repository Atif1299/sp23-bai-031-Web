Our Mission, Our Promise

Ethical and Beautiful

We are committed to quality products, produced ethically and sustainably with respect for people and planet.

We celebrate the age-old craft skills of our partners and - with the People Tree Foundation - promote initiatives to reinforce the viability of sustainable community producers. People Tree are proud to be the first international fashion brand certified by the World Fair Trade Association, with all pieces fully traceable in a transparent supply chain.

GOTS Certified Organic Cotton

We rarely run sales, but when we do, they never affect our makers.

We do our best to give you the best possible prices first time around, while still paying a fair price to our makers, weavers and farmers throughout the supply chain.

Our Fair Trade producers around the world are our top priority. When we hold a sale, this never affects their bottom line. Every time you buy from us, your money is directly invested back into them, supporting steady, skilled work around the world, and allowing us to keep bringing you environmentally-friendly clothing without compromise.
